⚙️ How It Works — 6 stages _▢✕

One pipeline. Six dumb-simple steps.

Run the whole thing, or pick the stages you want. Each one is independent.

STAGE 01
🔍

Discover

Scrapes Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Google Jobs + 48 Workday portals + 30 career sites. Dedupes everything.

STAGE 02
📑

Enrich

Opens each posting and pulls the full job description — structured data first, AI fallback for weird layouts.

STAGE 03
📊

Score

AI rates every job 1-10 against your resume and preferences. Only strong matches move forward.

STAGE 04
✂️

Tailor

Rewrites your resume per job — reorders, re-emphasizes, adds keywords. Your facts stay exact. Never fabricates.

STAGE 05
✉️

Cover Letter

Writes a targeted cover letter for each role and exports clean PDFs ready to send.

STAGE 06
🤖

Auto-Apply

Drives a real Chrome browser, fills the form, uploads your docs, answers screening questions, and submits.
